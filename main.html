Kyoto - A Timeless Beauty

Kyoto, Japan


Nestled in the heart of Japan, Kyoto is a city rich in history, tradition, and natural beauty. Known for its classical Buddhist temples, stunning gardens, imperial palaces, and traditional wooden houses, Kyoto is a serene blend of the old and new that enchants every traveller.

  1. Fushimi Inari Shrine – Famous for its thousands of vermilion torii gates.
  2. Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion) – A Zen temple whose top two floors are completely covered in gold leaf.
  3. Arashiyama Bamboo Grove – A walk through this mesmerising bamboo forest is truly magical.
  4. Kiyomizu-dera Temple – Known for its wooden stage and panoramic city views.
  5. Gion District – The historic area where one might spot a real Geisha in the evening.
  • Matcha (Green Tea) – Enjoyed in many forms, from lattes to desserts.
  • Kaiseki Ryori – A traditional multi-course meal that is both elegant and seasonal.
  • Yudofu – A hot pot dish of tofu simmered in light broth, perfect for winter.
  • Yatsuhashi – A popular Kyoto sweet made of cinnamon-flavoured rice flour.
  • Saba-zushi – A unique Kyoto-style mackerel sushi.
